Review of Ted

Nataly AquinoFebruary 08, 2023

Ted is, at heart, a relatively simple and predictable adventure comedy/buddy movie, It will have you falling off your seats with laughter.This movie has been one of my favorites since it came out ,Mark Wahlberg makes the movie complete with his great comedian acting skills and plus his beastfriend in the movie is a live teddy bear can't get any better then that. This was a ten out of ten movie.

Trivia
  • This was originally going to be an animated tv series.
  • Ted's "I Love You" function is voiced by Tara Strong .
  • The idea of Donny dancing was Giovanni Ribisi's
